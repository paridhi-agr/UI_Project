This data sets is currently for the restaurants in Las Vegas, Tempe and some parts of New York.

You can Type any of these regions/cities in the text field and Zoom into the region to easily view all the clustered markers in the Google Maps over that region.

You can use the draw tool to select the area over any region, and the region will only show the restaurants that falls in that region. This makes our search easier if we want to filter down our restaurant hunt to a confined area.

Lastly you can click on any of the restaurant markers to view the name, city, ratings and reviews of that particular restaurant and on top of that, you can actually view the 3D image of the restaurant itself via the Google Street View interface.
